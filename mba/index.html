Our MBA Program

Our MBA Program is a transformative 2-year journey that constantly innovates to meet the needs of students, industry and society at large via high-practical business content, world-class faculty, essential industry adjuncts, and cutting-edge pedagogy. The integrated learning between verticals and the intense, collaborative, and character-moulding experience of our MBA program, along with the localized knowledge makes it a unique offering for students from all across Pakistan. Our placement support and linkages across industries enable us to give our students the required platform to take off into the practical world.

The MBA Program hence imparts innovative business education that becomes life-long learning while keeping in view character development and personal ethics in the leaders of tomorrow. Our vision is to develop professionals who excel in operations yet never lose focus on ethical decision making and management.

The KSBL Benefit

KSBL MBA Program is a holistic offering that equips you with the skills and knowledge you need to have a securer future. We aim to provide an enriched educational experience through cutting edge teaching methodologies, hands-on approach to learning, and active campus life.

We take pride in our placement track record. Within a span of only a few years, our graduates are going places and securing decision making roles across all sectors and functions. Our placement support and linkages across industries enable us to give our students the required platform to take off into the world.

Moreover, our range of teaching methodologies, from case studies to online simulations, all give students a very hands-on learning experience.

MBA Curriculum

KSBL MBA program incorporates strategic teaching techniques with an emphasis on the latest business education, localized content and innovation, all of which is relevant for students planning their careers across various fields and disciplines.

The program entails 30 credit hour of teaching over the course of 1.5 years. Those with 16 years of education in other disciplines are required to take 30 additional credit hours with foundation and intermediate level courses to complete the KSBL MBA.
