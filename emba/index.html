The Executive MBA Program

The KSBL Executive MBA (EMBA) program is an opportunity for mid-career managers and entrepreneurs to prepare for future leadership roles that require sophisticated functional expertise and sound management capabilities. Our programs allow aspiring professionals to acquire advanced business education while pursuing their careers. Having innovation and futurism at its core, an EMBA enables you to perform optimally in cross-cultural and interdepartmental teams, develop your unique skill set, and thrive in the future.

This program is designed to stimulate an entrepreneurial mindset so you graduate bearing market readiness and bearing wholesome perspectives.

Preparing for the Future

KSBL Executive MBA program is carefully curated to meet the educational needs of working professionals across sectors, functions and regions - all through blended learning and innovation. This learning agility gives our EMBA participants access to a unique ecosystem - a network of peers, international faculty, opinion leaders and mentors.

EMBA Curriculum

KSBL's Executive MBA program is designed connecting the latest management studies with real-world scenarios, and entails 30 credit hour of teaching.

Those with 16 years of education in other disciplines are required to take 30 additional credit hours of foundation courses to successfully complete the KSBL EMBA Program.
